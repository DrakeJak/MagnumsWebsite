COVID-19 Policies

This is to be completed by ONE member of each group before you be seated and is required for contact tracing. Some other things to note before visiting our establishment:

  • Masks are required for picking up take-out orders & bathroom use Masks are available for purchase depending on availability. Masks are available for purchase at Magnums while supplies last.

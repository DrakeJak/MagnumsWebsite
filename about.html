Welcome to Magnums!

Magnums Bar & Eatery is an independently owned, family run bar. Now open 6 days a week for dinning in and takeout, our staff are working hard to ensure that you are still able to enjoy the patio season during these difficult times. Magnums Bar & Eatery was founded over 30 years ago by the Jakobsons family. Having a unique casual dining experience allows for customers to celebrate any occasion, whether it be a birthday or just an evening out with friends.
